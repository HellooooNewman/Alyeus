Alyeus

Alyeus (Ah-Lie-Us) is a progressive rock band from London, Ontario whose influences include Tool, Mastodon, and Thrice.

Taking guidance from bands like Rush and Coheed & Cambria, their debut album, Forty Days at Sea, will be the first release in a trilogy of concept albums that chronicle a story of war, a struggle for existence and a well kept secret.

Forty Days of Night

Released - 2013

Forty Days at Sea is the first release (but actually part two) of a three part series of concept albums. Part one will be released next, followed by part three.

We could not have done this without the help of those who donated to us through Indiegogo. It's hard to find the words to thank you, because "thank you" just seems so insufficient. You've made the dreams of four Canadian kids come true, and we are forever grateful. We'd also like to thank our family, our friends and those around us in Southern Ontario who have showed us support and came out to our shows.

Our Story

We're Entirely Concept Based! That's right, ENTIRELY! We're not talking one album, or a double CD release, or an EP...nope, entirely! Every song and Publication we release is in somehow tied into this detailed storyline we've conjured! Pretty cool eh?

That's called Forty Days at Sea, and that's where our story begins...for now!
